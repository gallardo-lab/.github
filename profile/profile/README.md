# Gallardo Lab

**Molecular oncology and targeted nanomedicine** at the
School of Medicine, Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León (UANL),
based at the Oncology Service of Hospital Universitario
"Dr. José Eleuterio González" — Monterrey, México.

## Research lines

- **MYC/MAX biology** — competitive blockade strategies and
  downstream pathway profiling for oncogenic transcriptional programs.
- **HER2+ targeted nanomedicine** — receptor-guided delivery
  platforms (aptamer-coated AuNPs and polymeric NPs) carrying
  mRNAs, miRNAs, antimiRs, and peptide constructs.
- **Post-GWAS functional annotation** — reproducible pipelines
  for type 2 diabetes and metabolic loci (e.g., RPTOR/rs12950541).
- **Breast cancer genetics in Northeastern Mexico** —
  gene–environment interactions, ACMG/CAP variant interpretation.
